HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or Solflare by going to phantom.com or solflare.com and create a Solana wallet.

Get Some SOL

To buy Quiet PePe, you need SOL in your wallet. Purchase SOL on Phantom or transfer from another wallet. You can also buy from an exchange and send it to your Phantom wallet.

Swap to Quiet PePe

Go to Raydium and input the contract address to swap.
